Parkville

Switch to Southbank

  • University Square

    A green retreat in the heart of the university precinct, with a central avenue leading to the Law building. The park is surrounded by the Graduate School of Business and Economics to the right, Melbourne Business School to the left and the main university campus on Grattan Street.

    AUDIO GUIDE

  • South Lawn

    A favourite amongst students in summer, South lawn is a hive of activity - relax in the sun with some friends, study in the shade of our magnificent trees or join a club at one of the regularly held barbecue lunches.

    AUDIO GUIDE

  • Baillieu Library

    The Baillieu Library is one of the largest in Australia, with over 3.6 million books and an array of unique study spaces for students.

    AUDIO GUIDE

  • Clocktower/Old Quad

    The most photographed landmark at the University of Melbourne is a five-level turreted structure that rises above Old Arts, overlooking South Lawn. It sits adjacent to the Old Quadrangle - our oldest building, featuring striking cloistered ceilings.

    AUDIO GUIDE

  • Wilson Hall

    The original Wilson Hall burnt down in 1952 and was replaced with the current design in 1956. This is the traditional place for graduation ceremonies, but exams are regularly held here too.

    AUDIO GUIDE

  • Melbourne School of Design

    Home to the school of Architecture, Building and Planning, the MSD is a multi-award winning, living, pedagogical building that sets a new global standard for education design.

    AUDIO GUIDE

  • Union Building

    A central hub for students, incorporating student services, retail, a food court, and lounge access.
